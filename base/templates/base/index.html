Working as a Technician

Despite the rising demand for IT technicians and repairmen, individuals who possess these skills still struggle to penetrate the market. You might be one of them; Highly skilled, with a little extra time, and an interest in doing what you do best. At Glitchtec, we believe that if you have these skills, you should be able to monetize them. Our platform provides you with an alternative form of income in a world where it is increasingly difficult to find jobs that utilize your skillset. High quality and affordable IT support should not come at the expense of those providing support. Working as a technician on our platform, you will set your own hours, choose which tickets you will solve, and get paid per fix, not by the hour. This means that anyone with IT skills can make money on our platform, whether you have had years of experience in IT support or only have skills with one specific software or type of IT problem, and work on our platform full time or only a few hours a week. Our platform allows you to customize your profile with relative skills, preferred hours and much more, we will send you notifications when customers post tickets relevant to your profile and/or previous tickets you have solved, eliminating the need to search through tickets in order to find ones relevant to your skills. At Glitchtec we strive to give our customers the highest quality service at an affordable price, but we will never come at a sacrifice the well-being of our technicians. Rather, we encourage technicians to build personal relationships with customers by giving monetary incentives to both technicians and customers for repeat customer-technician pairs. We believe that these relationships will help technicians give top quality service to customers they have previously worked with, and is the most effective way to build quality service and trust. Finally, unlike many gig-economy platforms, we believe that exceptional performance should be rewarded with more than a good rating on your profile. We aim to give technicians not only a means to monetize their skills, but also opportunities and resources for career advancement.
